Digital Marketing

OUTBOUND & INBOUND

Outbound Marketing

This is a technique where you reach out to potential customers

Step 1: Identifying the right kind of customers

Knowing your customers is critical; you must understand the everyday behaviors of your target customer - interests, lifestyles, income, ethnicity, language, hobbies and more.

Step 2: Getting infront of potential customers

This is every business' most difficult challenge. Among the many options available, the easiest would be to pay Google and Facebook to get you in front of potential customers.

Step 3: Grabbing attention

With any medium you employ, you will have mostly between 2 - 5 seconds to catch the attention of any potential customer. This requires professional artistry to produce quality art that can grab attention. Video is out performing the other media types at the moment.

Step 4: Creating a great first impression

What potential customers think of your company and their decision to do business with you or not is determined by their first impressions of you. People judge your business by your website, your social media accounts, reviews and the content you put out there.

Step 5: Bringing them in

This is the ultimate goal. By the time you get to this stage, some people may have already come to the decision to use your services, while others will require a little nudge.

Inbound Marketing

This technique allows you to show up before your competitors

Step 1: Identify What your customers search often

It all starts by researching popular search terms your potential customers type into search engines like Google.

Step 2: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

This is basically optimizing your online content following the best practices that are recomended by search engines. If you optimise your content better than your competitor, Google will favor your business over your competitors'.

Step 3: Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

Sometimes, no matter how much you optimise the content on your website, it is very hard to win over some keywords. The alternative would be get into an agreement with Google and pay a certain amount to be shown at the top.

Step 4: Creating a great first impression

What potential customers think of your company and their decision to do business with you or not is determined by their first impressions of you. People judge your business by your website, your social media accounts, reviews and the content you put out there.

Step 5: Bringing them in

This is the ultimate goal. By the time you get to this stage, some people may have already come to the decision to use your services, while others will require a little nudge. You use your website and other medias to nudge them a bit more.
